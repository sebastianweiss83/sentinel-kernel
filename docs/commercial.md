# Commercial support

The `sentinel-kernel` layer is and will remain **Apache 2.0, forever**.
No BSL, no open-core, no paid features, no relicensing.

That commitment is architectural, not marketing. The three invariants
in [CLAUDE.md](../CLAUDE.md) (no US CLOUD Act exposure in the critical
path, air-gapped must always work, Apache 2.0 forever) apply to every
release.

The commercial offering below exists because regulated organisations
— banks, insurers, defence contractors, healthcare providers,
critical infrastructure operators — need accountable humans behind a
piece of infrastructure they are about to put in front of an auditor.
The open-source layer is the product. Support is the relationship
around it.

## What is available

- **Deployment assistance.** On-premise, EU-sovereign, and air-gapped
  installation support, including VS-NfD-capable environments.
- **Audit preparation.** Review of your decision traces, evidence
  packs, manifesto, and policy layer against the specific articles of
  the EU AI Act, NIS2, DORA, or BSI IT-Grundschutz that apply to your
  organisation.
- **BSI pre-engagement support.** Assistance with the pre-engagement
  package described in
  [docs/bsi-pre-engagement/README.md](bsi-pre-engagement/README.md).
- **Custom policy libraries.** Sector-specific policy bundles
  (procurement, clinical, defence, financial) developed against your
  actual risk controls.
- **Incident response.** Help investigating a specific decision,
  reconstructing state from traces, and preparing the technical
  portion of an incident report for a regulator.
- **Service-level agreements.** Response times for security advisories,
  coordinated disclosure, and critical bug fixes against a named
  release line.

## What is explicitly not available

- A hosted SaaS version of Sentinel. There is no cloud product, and
  there will not be one. That is the whole point.
- A commercial fork of the core layer with exclusive features. Every
  feature that exists lives in this repository under Apache 2.0.
- Any arrangement that would make US-owned infrastructure part of a
  customer's critical path.

## How to get in touch

This is an early-stage project and there is no commercial sales org
yet. The honest way to reach us today is to open a GitHub issue on
this repository describing what you need, or to contact the
maintainers through the channels listed in
[GOVERNANCE.md](../GOVERNANCE.md).

Real pricing, real contracts, and a real company structure will
follow once there are enough design partners to justify them. Until
then, the offer above is handled case by case, transparently, and in
writing.
